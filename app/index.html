About

techNIEks, the annual techno-cultural extravaganza, organized by the National Institute of Engineering, Mysore, is scheduled to be held from the 12th to 14th March 2015, based on the theme "Coasts of India". techNIEks has been a grand success over the years, bringing together the youth from many institutes all over India.
